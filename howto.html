How to Use

  • 1

    Upload Your Video

    Drag and drop your video file (MP4, WebM, Ogg) into the upload area or click the "Select Video" button.

  • 2

    Select Extraction Mode

    First Frame: Extracts only the very first frame of the video.
    All Frames: Extracts multiple frames based on the FPS (frames per second) slider.

  • 3

    Configure Settings

    Adjust the FPS Slider to control how many images are captured per second. Choose your preferred output format (PNG or JPEG).

  • 4

    Extract & Download

    Click "Extract Frame" and wait for the process to finish. Once done, you can download your images individually or as a ZIP file.

Pro Tip: For large videos, try extracting a small 10-second clip first to test your settings!
